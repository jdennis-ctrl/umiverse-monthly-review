Feature Focus: Multi-Org Filtering - The Why

Problem to production - How we got here

Multi-Org Filtering was raised by stakeholders during a workshop focused on another issue, this was subsequently shaped through lots of collaboration to help improve internal processes on reporting on clients and their users.

🌟
Oct 2025
Raised in ideation workshop▼
Held an ideation session with stakeholders across various areas of the business, primarily focused on Bulk User Creation. Multi-org filtering was raised as something that would significantly improve reporting on orgs and users.
📊
Nov 2025
Defining the scope▼
Initial requirements were exchanged and defined via Slack / email & calls to align on functionality needed. Concept designs were made to help confirm the approach.
🎬
Dec 2025 Onwards
Design playbacks▼
Product Design and I provided updates at GTM Show & Tell meetings. Design also held dedicated demo and playback sessions with key stakeholders.
✅
Mar 2026
Signed off, built and launched▼
Once designs were signed off we moved into development. A final demo was held with stakeholders to confirm sign-off before going live.

What this replaced

Manual filtering by one org at a time - for large clients like Publicis this was a long winded flow
Exporting multiple separate CSV files of users that were being filtered per org - higher error risk
Manually consolidating the results of these exports outside of Admin into one master file
A significant amount of repeated manual effort for what should be a more simpler & routine task

Feature Focus: Multi-Org Filtering - What We Delivered

Solution we delivered

Here is what is live in Admin today since we released this first phase middle of March. Also highlighted is a quick post release fix that was identified as a bug upon users engaging with new tool in production.

✓

Multi-select orgs in new modal

Select multiple orgs to filter users in a single action from a new searchable overlay - no more one at a time.

✓

"Select All" after filtering

Bulk-select all results returned by an org search, or use infinite scroll to find exact required orgs to help filter users.

✓

Cross-client org selection in one go

Pick organisations from different clients in a single filter action without losing previous selections.

✓

Live counter showing total orgs selected

A running total shown next to the Save filter button so you always know how many orgs are in scope.

🐛

Post-release fix - an infinite scroll edge case identified by QA was resolved. Results now load reliably at scale.

Feature Focus: Multi-Org Filtering - Feedback & What's Next

Listening to feedback - what we're improving

Following release we have been collecting feedback from end users. Here's what we are working on and some further context on an issue that may have been encountered.

Improvements in progress

Fixing search by ID - improving how ID search behaves (Designed - Awaiting Development)
Filtering by plan - allowing users to narrow results by plan type within the org filter (In Analysis)
Long term clean-up of duplicated organisations which has caused confusion (In Analysis)

A note on duplicate organisations

The new filtering surfaces all matching organisations more transparently, which can make duplicates more visible than before. We have a wider clean-up planned - there are currently 70,000 organisations without users - and appreciate this can make certain searches less straightforward in the meantime.
